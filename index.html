About

I work where intent meets infrastructure.

I'm a product designer at Meta on the Ads Manager App, focused on a deceptively hard problem: making sure advertisers can actually be reached when it matters. My work lives at the seam between a native app where intent happens and a web verification stack that owns consent and compliance — so most of the craft is in continuity, truthful state, and decisions that hold up under cross-functional scrutiny. I lead these efforts end to end, from exploration and pattern research to working prototypes and the alignment that gets a design approved.

Approach

How I work.

01

Decide the variables separately.

Affordance and status are different questions — I isolate each, weigh it against real principles (Fitts, Norman, Jakob), then decide deliberately instead of by default.

02

Convention informs; the team decides.

I bring the platform research — what FB, IG, and iOS actually do — so trade-offs are understood, not assumed. The pattern analysis earns the decision; it doesn't override it.

03

Prototype the seams.

The handoffs are where products break. I build the full flow end to end — backdrops, state machines, error states — so eng and content align on real behavior, not static comps.

04

Keep state truthful.

Not added → Pending confirmation → Verified. State should always tell the user exactly where they are, and pull them back to finish what they started.
