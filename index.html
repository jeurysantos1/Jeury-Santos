Approach

How I work — research to native prototype.

A user-centered process, accelerated end to end with AI agents — from discovery and insight to a native-grade, interactive prototype.

01

Research, accelerated by AI

I put AI agents to work on discovery — running and synthesizing research, or partnering with researchers — to surface real user pain points fast. Design starts from evidence, not assumptions.

02

Insight, then many approaches

I turn research into sharp insight, then use AI agents to rapidly produce multiple directions — pressure-tested against proven UX patterns — and align the team on the most feasible one.

03

Crafted pixel to pixel

I take the chosen direction all the way down — spacing, states, motion, and edge cases — so the design is production-ready, not a rough comp.

04

Native-grade prototypes

I bring the design to life as a native-grade, interactive prototype, so engineering and content align on real behavior and the seams — not static screens.
